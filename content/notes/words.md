# Words

concise - лаконичный
essential - существенный, основной
reside - находится
incur - навлечь на себя

denote - обозначает
reside - проживать, находится, пребывать, быть свойственным

derivation:
    1. the obtaining or developing of something from a source or origin.
    2. Mathematics: the process of deducing a new formula, theorem, 
        etc., from previously accepted statements.

preemptive - serving or intended to preempt or forestall something, especially to prevent attack by disabling the enemy

correspondingly - in an analogous or equivalent way

equitable - fair and impartial

confer - 1) grant or bestow (a title, degree, benefit, or right); 2) have discussions; exchange opinions

thereby - by that means; as a result of that

adversely - in a way that prevents success or development; harmfully or unfavorably (неблагоприятно)

accustomed - customary; usual

resemblance - сходство

albeit - although (хотя, тем не менее)

thus - таким образом

Supplementary - completing or enhancing something (дополнительный)

abide - accept or act in accordance with (a rule, decision or recommendation - соблюдать)

corresponds - have a close similarity; match or agree almost exactly

envisaging - contemplate or conceive of as a possibility or a desirable future event (представлять себе, предвидеть)
